# Brand Expert Enhancement Plan: K-7 BRAND ARCHITECT

This document outlines the strategic roadmap to transform the **K-7 Brand Architect** from a generative tool into a **Non-Negotiable AGI Partner** for founders.

## 1. The "Non-Negotiable" Philosophy
To make the agent indispensable, we transition from **"Prompt & Response"** to **"Insight & Execution"**. The agent must provide assets that would normally take a branding agency 4 weeks, in under 40 seconds.

---

## 2. Phase 1: Deep Semantic Synthesis (Logic Layer)
**Goal**: Elevate the output from "Text" to "Blueprint".

- [ ] **Neural Gap Analysis**: Update `gemini.ts` to perform a competitor analysis based on the niche.
- [ ] **Psychographic Mapping**: Generate a "User Persona Map" (Neuro-Target) including pain points and behavioral triggers.
- [ ] **Dynamic Value Proposition**: Automate the creation of a "Hero Header" and "Sub-text" for the founder's landing page.

---

## 3. Phase 2: Visual Identity Engine (Creative Layer)
**Goal**: Provide tangible visual assets.

- [ ] **Neural Moodboarding**: Integrate an image generation endpoint (DALL-E 3) to create a visual "Brand Atmosphere" (4 images: Color Palette, Texture, Lifestyle, Typography).
- [ ] **Design Token Export**: Automatically generate a `theme.json` or `tailwind.config.js` snippet based on the brand's DNA.
- [ ] **3D Visualization**: Enhance the `RoboticAgentVisual` in `HireAgent.tsx` to react to specific brand colors selected by the AI.

---

## 4. Phase 3: The "Neural Call" (Interaction Layer)
**Goal**: Humanize the AGI experience through multi-modal input.

- [ ] **Acoustic Calibration**: Implement a real microphone listener (Web Audio API) that captures the founder's pitch and uses AI to analyze "Tone of Voice" (Calm, Disruptive, Elite).
- [ ] **Interactive Terminal**: Replace standard input fields with a "Neural Terminal" that mimics a real-time data uplink.
- [ ] **Voice Synthesis**: (Optional) Use ElevenLabs to give K-7 a distinct, premium voice during the "Deployment Phase".

---

## 5. Phase 4: Executive Export Protocol (Delivery Layer)
**Goal**: Provide professional, shareable deliverables.

- [ ] **PDF Constitution**: Implement `jsPDF` to generate a 5-page "Brand Constitution" PDF including:
    - Mission & Vision
    - Visual Identity (Colors/Typography)
    - Tone of Voice Guidelines
    - 6-Month Roadmap
- [ ] **Markdown Vault**: One-click download of the `README.md` for the new project.

---

## 6. Design Consistencies
To maintain the "PromptBook" elite aesthetic:
*   **Typography**: Stick to `Display` (Large, Tracking-Tighter) for headings and `Mono` (Small, Tracking-Widest) for labels.
*   **Color Palette**: Use `#ffcc00` (Gold/Caution) for K-7 and `#00ccff` (Neon Blue/Sync) for S-4.
*   **Micro-Animations**: Every "Next Sequence" click should trigger a GSAP "Scanline" across the UI.
